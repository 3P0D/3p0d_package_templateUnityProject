Here is a proposition of nomenclature for your FX sprites:


sprt_fx_nameName_var_00_a_spriteType


This nomenclature should be used for your FX sprites such as particles, decals, etc.
Add the sprite type only if it is a spritesheet or an atlas.


Examples: 
> sprt_fx_roundedStar_tiny_spritesheet
> sprt_fx_heart
