Here is a proposition of nomenclature for your sprites:


sprt_target_nameName_var_00_a_spriteType


This nomenclature should be used for your assets in a 2D game such as atlasses, spritesheets, etc.
The sprite type would be the either atlas, spritesheet or single.


Examples: 
> sprt_item_currency_goldcoin_single
> sprt_enemy_sprite_orcWarlock_rank1_red_spritesheet
> sprt_bg_forest_trees_autumn_A_atlas
